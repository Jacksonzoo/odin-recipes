Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e Pepe is a creamy pasta dish from Italy. Although it tastes very good when I make it, it is hard to give a description on something I am not knowledgeable about.

Ingredients

  • Bucatini (or Spaghetti if you don't have)
  • Black Pepper
  • Unsalted Grass-Fed Butter
  • Freshly fine grated Pecorino Romano
  • Kosher or Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil and generously season with salt
  2. Add your pasta and cook until just before "al dente" (usually a minute before the suggested time on the box)
  3. Drain your pasta, and save roughly a cup of the pasta water
  4. Heat dry skillet to medium-high heat
  5. Freshly crack some peppers onto skillet, and let the grounded black peppers get "toasty"
  6. Add half of your pasta water into the skillet and a knob of butter to emulsify the liquid
  7. After cooking for a short period of time, add in the pasta and begin to stir
  8. Toss the pasta back and forth to mix into the 'sauce'
  9. Add in the freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese until the sauce thickens up more
  10. Add more pasta water if necessary
  11. Plate and garnish with more cheese
