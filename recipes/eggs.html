Soft Scrambled Eggs

Description

The perfect way to make eggs in the morning for a filling way to start your day. These soft scrambled eggs will literally make you look at eggs a different way.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 knobs of grass-fed butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green Onion Chives

Steps

  1. Crack 3 Eggs into cold skillet
  2. Add one knob of butter into skillet and turn the heat to medium-high
  3. Keep stirring the eggs and butter in circles in both directions
  4. When first knob of butter is almost fully melted, add the second knob
  5. Keep stirring to prevent big curdles from forming
  6. Repeat the fourth step with the last knob of butter
  7. When eggs are begin to form silky shape and micro-curds, take the skillet off the heat and season with salt and pepper to taste
  8. Stir back on heat to incorporate seasoning and desired doneness
  9. When desired doneness is reached, transfer your eggs to plate and garnish with fresh chopped green onions
