Steak

Description

Steak is one of the most popular dinners to make. Ranging from simple ingredients to complex flavors, steaks are a vehicle for flavor regardless of the material (as long as you do it correctly).

ingredients

  • Steak of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Grass-fed Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet to high heat
  2. Dry your steak with a paper towel
  3. Add a little olive oil to both sides of the steak (so that seasoning will stick)
  4. Season steak with salt and pepper to your own liking on both sides
  5. When skillet is to temp, place steak on skillet away from you
  6. Sear for 2-3 minutes, depending on the size of steak, do not move too much
  7. After time is up, flip and cook for another 2 minutes
  8. Turk heat to medium, take skillet off the heat while you gather butter, crushed garlic, thyme, and rosemary
  9. Put the gathered ingredients in with your steak and start to baste the steak for one minute
  10. Set steak aside, let it rest for at least 5 minutes, preferably 10 minutes, before cutting
  11. Plate and Serve
